Who am I?

I’m passionate about making a positive difference in the world. My biggest focuses are advancing equity in the education system by empowering our most vulnerable and struggling students.

I’ve been a Cross Country and Track athlete since 8th grade. I enjoy pushing myself through difficult workouts, and I’ve learned so much over the years on persevering. I’m really excited to see how I’ve improved to the all-Wesco runner and team captain that I am today. I’ve also played the piano competitively since I was 5. I’m an enthusiastic singer and songwriter, with more than 9 years of amazing learning in choir and opera. In my free time, I love to read and go hiking.
